Nature of the position:

Delivery is our core business. The bags and packages are pre-made by our colleagues in the depot, so that all you have to do is load them in and you can concentrate completely on the delivery. You drive the delivery truck through motorways, country roads and city traffic and deliver the packages to private and business addresses using an automated navigation system.

Job Overview

Delivery of parcels to customer addresses, light parcels up to 15 kg at short distances daily itinerary prepared in advance. Permanent area and modern route optimization system (with navigation). You work with software, which describes the deliveries and the location of the addresses. It works in mostly rural areas, mainly houses. The stops and volume of packages varies depending from the period and the workload of the depot - it works between 7-9 hours a day.

Job Overview

You’ll be part of the Amazon warehouse team that handles the big stuff – those unique customer orders that weigh over 40kg: big screen TVs, furniture, appliances, and more. You’ll be trained on how to use technology to handle these heavy bulk items. Even if you haven’t been a “power” player before, that won’t limit you from gaining valuable job experience because – depending on the site – you may be trained to be a heavy equipment operator, using powered industrial trucks (PIT) such as a forklift or pallet driver. Many shifts will have you at the helm of our power equipment getting orders ready for customers, but other times you’ll be on the warehouse floor of our fulfillment centers, sortation centers, or delivery stations working with your teammates, tackling those hands-on items that need expert handling before they are sent along their delivery route.
